A.D.D.(Anthony Damn Duncan)

Comedian/Entertainer/Artist

What happens when a privledged and sheltered child with Autism and A.D.D. has to live on the streets? How did he survive? He had a kid as a teen grew up working every minimun wage job under the sun, while interacting with gangs, juggalos, aggressive house less, promiscious blind dates, stoner fetsihes, and weight loss by becoming a Comedian/Entertainer! Anthony Damn Duncan also known as A.D.D. is a eccentric entertaining young man whose been in the industry for five years sharing some of the biggest stages with seasoned comics such as Mo Alexander, Alan Bromwell, Josh Blue, Mo Vida, Rick Bryan, Derrick Rush, Anthony Crawford, and Stephen Ayeegi.
